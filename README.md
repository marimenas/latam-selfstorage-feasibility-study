# Self-Storage Market Feasibility Study — Honduras & Central America

**Project type:** Market Research & Strategic Feasibility  
**Duration:** 4 months  
**Client:** Confidential (private investor, Honduras)  
**Conducted by:** Sandra Marisol Mena Segurado — MMarketing  
**Languages:** Spanish / English  

## Project Overview

End-to-end feasibility study assessing the viability of launching 
a self-storage business in Honduras, benchmarked against the 
mature US self-storage market and regional competitors across 
Central America.

This project combined primary field research (on-site visits in 
Miami, FL), secondary market analysis, demand modelling, and 
strategic recommendations delivered to the client as an 
executive report.

## Research Objectives

- Determine market viability for self-storage in Honduras
- Benchmark against established US operators
- Map competitive landscape across Central America
- Model demand and pricing strategy
- Recommend go/no-go with supporting data

## 🔍 Methodology

### Phase 1 — US Field Research (Miami, FL)
- On-site visits to **7 self-storage facilities** across Miami
- Assessed: unit mix, pricing tiers, occupancy signals, 
  customer demographics, digital marketing presence, 
  amenities, and operational model
- Conducted informal interviews with facility staff
- Documented findings in structured comparison matrix

### Phase 2 — Central American Market Analysis
- Competitive mapping: Honduras, Guatemala, El Salvador, 
  Costa Rica
- Identified existing operators, pricing benchmarks, 
  and market gaps
- Analysed urbanisation trends, housing density, and 
  consumer behaviour patterns relevant to storage demand

### Phase 3 — Demand Modelling & Financial Projections
- Collaborated with data management specialist for 
  quantitative analysis
- Modelled demand scenarios (conservative / base / optimistic)
- Developed unit-mix recommendations and pricing strategy
- Estimated break-even timeline and ROI projections

### Phase 4 — Executive Report & Recommendations
- Delivered full feasibility report to client
- Included go/no-go recommendation with supporting rationale
- Presented digital marketing strategy for market entry
